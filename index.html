Alan Zamora was born and raised in the Bay Area. He is currently a Visual Communications Student at SFSU. He is working on improving his Graphic Design skills while also doing a minor in Video Game Studies. Alan hopes to continue learning and finishing his degree at SFSU. He plans to transfer to SJSU to attend the Masters of Library Science. He plans to continue working for his Library County System, where he runs his own Makerspace. He runs and teaches different equipment like 3D Printers, Laser cutters, Virtual Reality Headsets, and much more. Alan enjoys working and helping the community in any way he can. With a focus on Design and making information accessible Alan hopes to make Makerspaces more available to the community.

When Alan is not working in the Makerspace or attending school, he enjoys many hobbies! He enjoys tinkering with different and new technology. He also enjoys Mountain Biking, Photography, and Hiking! He has recently, got into running and traveling. He hopes to improve his fitness by running and attending a 10K in the near future! He also is planning on traveling more often and adventuring around the world! With his different work, life, and want to help the community he hopes he can make a meaningful change to assist the community. He wants to design, and create more accessible resources for designers and makers to learn and develop their own skills! Please join Alan in his journey of Graphic Design and Making!
